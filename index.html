can a lovesong start a revolution? we know it can

In may 2023 the song “Dammi Falestini’ by Mohammed Assaf was removed by spotify and apple music because zionists claimed it was antisemetic. Mind you, there is no mention of the Jewish community in this song. By removing this song while Palestine is opressed by the state of israel, this song was made political.

how?

Our aim is for you to play this song every day at 10.00 AM in your country. We need our voices to be heard! The more people playing this song at the same time every day, the more powerful it will be. download the song here. and keep your browser open so the song will automatically play at 10 am.
(Preferably put it on speakers)

we do not go against any laws by protesting this way but they won't be able to ignore entire countries playing the same song at the same time every day.
